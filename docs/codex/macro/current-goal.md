## Plain English

The system is not trying to produce "picks." It is trying to make every possible trade comparable on one ledger:

- What exact contract would we buy or avoid?
- What is the real executable break-even after price, spread, and fees?
- What calibrated probability do we have from our own model or evidence?
- Is the gap real after timing, mapping, resolution, data-quality, and calibration gates?
- If not, what exact evidence is missing?

Until a row clears those gates, it is research evidence, not an actionable edge.
